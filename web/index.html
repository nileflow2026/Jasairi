Learning that adapts to every child

JASIRI is an AI-powered assistive learning platform designed for children with Down syndrome — calm, playful, and built around each child's unique strengths.

Explore Features Privacy Policy

What JASIRI offers

Everything a child needs to grow with confidence

Each feature is designed to reduce cognitive load, reward effort, and build real learning outcomes.

🎮

Serious Games

Short, engaging educational games covering memory, pattern recognition, sequencing, and motor coordination.

🤖

AI Personalisation

Rule-based AI adapts difficulty, recommends activities, and adjusts pacing to each child's unique learning rhythm.

🎨

Digital Art Studio

A calming canvas for finger painting, drawing, and emotional expression — with large, accessible controls.

📊

Caregiver Dashboard

Parents and teachers see clear progress reports, activity insights, and artwork — all in one simple view.

📶

Offline-First

Core learning experiences work without internet — essential for intermittent connectivity across Africa.

♿

Accessibility First

Large touch targets, visual communication, audio reinforcement, and minimal reading requirements throughout.
