Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: May 2026

📋 1. Introduction

JASIRI is an AI-powered assistive learning platform designed for children with Down syndrome. We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy and safety of every child who uses our platform. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

JASIRI is designed for children. All accounts are created and managed by a parent, guardian, teacher, or therapist — never directly by a child.

🌍 2. Who We Are

JASIRI is developed and operated by the JASIRI Team, based in Kenya. We serve families, schools, and therapists across Africa and beyond. Our mission is grounded in the Ubuntu philosophy: "We are because of each other."

📦 3. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide a safe, personalised learning experience.

  • Caregiver account details — name and email address, used for authentication.
  • Child profile details — first name and age range, used to personalise activities. We do not collect full birth dates.
  • Learning activity data — game scores, session duration, and activity preferences, used to adapt difficulty and recommend activities.
  • Artwork — drawings created in the Art Studio, stored locally and optionally synced to the caregiver's account.
  • Device information — basic device type and OS version, used for performance optimisation. We do not track device identifiers.
We do not collect children's full names, photos, videos, location data, or any biometric information.

⚙️ 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Personalise learning activities and adapt difficulty levels for each child.
  • Enable caregivers to monitor progress and track learning milestones.
  • Improve app reliability and performance.
  • Send essential account notifications (e.g., email verification). We do not send marketing emails.

We never use children's data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

🧒 5. Children's Privacy

JASIRI is specifically designed for children with Down syndrome. We treat children's data with the highest level of care:

  • All accounts are created and controlled by a verified adult caregiver.
  • Children cannot create accounts, make purchases, or share content independently.
  • Caregivers can delete a child's profile and all associated data at any time.
  • We do not allow children to communicate with strangers or share content publicly.
  • We comply with child privacy principles aligned with COPPA and GDPR-K guidelines.

🔒 6. Data Storage & Security

Learning data is stored locally on the device first (offline-first architecture) and synced to our secure cloud backend powered by Appwrite when connectivity is available. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Stored data is access-controlled with role-based permissions — caregivers can only access their own children's data.

  • We do not expose admin keys or secrets in the app.
  • Sensitive operations are handled exclusively on our backend servers.
  • We perform regular security reviews of our infrastructure.

🤝 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties, except:

  • Service providers — infrastructure partners (e.g., cloud hosting) who are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements — if required by applicable law or to protect the safety of a child.

We never share children's data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing platforms.

🗓️ 8. Data Retention

We retain account and learning data for as long as the caregiver's account is active. When a caregiver deletes a child profile, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days. When an account is fully deleted, all data is removed within 30 days.

✅ 9. Your Rights

As a caregiver or account holder, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you and your child.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

📝 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we make significant changes, we will notify caregivers via in-app notification or email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of JASIRI after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
